Horizontal Gene Transfer, 76 x 60 cm, inkjet print on forex, 2014/2015

Taking the superpostion and multiplication of the images of different single-cell organisms (diatoms) as a visual equivalent of the process of horizontal gene transfer between them, the work shows a fictional kind of (diatom) protocell. 

The lower image shows the work within the exhibition 'Nature happens.' at the German Maritime Museum, Bremerhaven in March 2015. For more information on the project 'Nature happens.' please visit:
www.rangsch.de/nature_happens.html
